My Approach

Take the initiative, sketch more ideas, focus on the problem, and help everyone succeed.

From the beginning of my career, I have been dedicated to understanding the root causes of systemic issues and breaking them down into smaller, more manageable pieces to find the best solution.

Growing up in the rural foothills of North Carolina, I developed a strong curiosity about how things work. I have always been fascinated by systems and mechanisms, and enjoy using my creativity to tackle new challenges in these areas. As the son of a computer scientist, I was encouraged from a young age to explore and understand both computer software and hardware. This curiosity and passion for problem-solving led me to pursue a career in the field.
