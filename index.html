About Me

Raised in North Carolina, explorer of Tennessee, friend to Kentucky, and friendly neighbor in Pennsylvania.

Coming up in the rural foothills of North Carolina, I was raised with a deep love for understanding how things operate. Systems and mechanisms have always interested me, and allowed me to apply my creativity in new and challenging ways. Son of a computer scientist, I was encouraged at young age to take apart and understand both computer software and hardware over most summers.
